About project

This project focused on solving a critical issue in a fintech app where users were mistakenly sending money to the wrong bank accounts—costing the business thousands of dollars weekly in chargebacks. By identifying the root cause through user interviews and flow analysis, I worked with the team to redesign the send-money flow, implement necessary validation checks, and eliminate a backend API bug. The outcome? Zero customer errors, zero chargebacks from this issue, and a more thoughtful, error-resistant experience.
